Our History

At Three Quarters, everything starts in the kitchen. Our restaurant was built on a love for real Italian cooking, the kind that fills the room with the aroma of simmering tomatoes, garlic, and fresh basil.

The name Three Quarters comes from our signature approach: three quarters tradition, one quarter creativity. Pizzas with perfectly blistered crusts, every dish is prepared with intention, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients we can find. Our story isn’t just about family, it's about food that brings people together, plate after plate, year after year.

The Founder

Giovanni — Founder
Born in Naples and a lifelong lover of food, Giovanni opened the Three Quarters with one goal: to bring Italian home cooking to everyone.
